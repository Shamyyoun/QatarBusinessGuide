In 2002, the Government of Egypt (GoE) enacted Law No. 83 for the year 2002 for establishing Economic Zones of a Special Nature. North-West Gulf of Suez was appointed as a Special Economic Zone (General Authority for Economic Zone North West of Gulf Suez) which is located in the Suez Governorate to be established by Presidential Decree no. 35 for the year 2003. SEZONE is the first economic zone with a special nature to be established in Egypt by law 83 of 2002 for Economic Zones of a Special Nature. Also is intended to provide an attractive environment for medium and light industries as well as logistics services.
